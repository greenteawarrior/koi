Koi Fish (CS307, hwk6 Creative Scene!)

Emily Wang, Fall 2014

Features of this iteration of the koi fish:

  • Bezier curves + Lathe geometries for the fish body and fins!!
  • Texture mapping with a lathe geometry
  • Coordinate transforms for placing the fins/eyes
  • Scene in a box
  • Ambient + Directional lights

Sources of texture images:

  • Purple scales
  • Shiny silver fins
  • Pond background, with this one in particular

Brainstorming ideas for the next iterations (perhaps some features for a koi pond final project!):

  • Geometry: Variations on koi fish shapes/sizes
  • Geometry: Binary subtraction to get a more flowy/curved tail fin
  • Animation: Fish swimming
  • Animation: Have multiple fish swimming together
  • Animation: Water ripple effects
  • Lighting: Consider eather/Water lighting in scene
  • Textures: Different textures/colors for multiple fish in scene
  • Scene: Add rocks (convex geometries maybe?) and/or plants
  • Camera: Different views of the pond
